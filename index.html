Travel Itineraries

3 Days in Paris

Day 1: Visit the Eiffel Tower and take a Seine River cruise.

Day 2: Explore the Louvre Museum and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Day 3: Stroll through Montmartre and visit Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

5 Days in Tokyo

Day 1: Explore Shibuya Crossing and Shinjuku.

Day 2: Visit Asakusa and the Senso-ji Temple.

Day 3: Enjoy Akihabara and Tokyo Tower.

Day 4: Discover Harajuku and Meiji Shrine.

Day 5: Take a day trip to Mt. Fuji.

4 Days in New York City

Day 1: Visit Times Square and Broadway.

Day 2: Explore Central Park and the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Day 3: Discover the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island.

Day 4: Walk through the High Line and visit the Chelsea Market.
